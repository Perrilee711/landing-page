Got questions?
We have answers.
Do I need to speak Chinese?
Nope. That's the whole point. We handle all the communication in both Mandarin and English. You just approve orders and focus on selling.
How do I send you product requests?
Slack, email, or our web form. Send us a product link (1688, Taobao, Alibaba, any Chinese supplier) or just describe what you're looking for. We'll get back to you within 24 hours with a quote.
What's your defect rate?
Less than 2% across all shipments. Our QC process catches most issues before they leave China. If a defective item still slips through, we'll handle the claim with the supplier and get a replacement organized.
What if I'm not satisfied with a supplier?
We'll find you a better one. Part of our service is vetting multiple suppliers to make sure you get the best quality and price. If a current supplier isn't performing, we flag it and source alternatives.
Is there a minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
That depends on the supplier. Some items have MOQs as low as 50 units, others are 500+. We'll let you know upfront so you can decide before placing any order. No surprises.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Monthly plans, no long-term contracts. Cancel whenever you want — no fees, no questions asked. We'll finish any orders already in progress at the time of cancellation.
How long does shipping take?
Air freight: 7-12 days door-to-door. Sea freight: 25-35 days. We'll recommend the best option based on your order size and timeline. All shipping includes tracking and insurance.
